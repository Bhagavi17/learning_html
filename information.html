Mastercard Inc.

Mastercard Inc. (stylized as MasterCard from 1979 to 2016 and as mastercard from 2016) is an American multinational payment card services corporation.

The company is headquartered in Purchase, New York. It provides payment transaction processing and other payment-related services, including travel-related payments and bookings.
